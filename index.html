About Synex

Synex Medical is dedicated to providing people with accurate information about how their bodies are functioning so they can be proactive about their health.

To accomplish this, we’re building the next generation of health monitoring technology. We’ve developed novel techniques based on magnetic resonance, the same underlying principle used in magnetic resonance imaging (MRI), that allow us to non-invasively and accurately measure critical blood metabolites like glucose, lactate and ketones. This technology will come in a highly miniaturized form-factor to allow for real-time measurements.
